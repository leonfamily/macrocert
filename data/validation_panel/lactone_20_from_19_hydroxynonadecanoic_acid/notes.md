# lactone_20_from_19_hydroxynonadecanoic_acid

Surrogate panel case ŌĆö 20-membered macrolactone from `OC(=O)CCCCCCCCCCCCCCCCCCO`.

The structure here is the *target product* (cyclized macrolactone),
not the literature compound; the chemistry is real (Mukaiyama 1976
and the Parenty 2013 review describe the omega-hydroxy acid series
explicitly) but the substrate is simplified for panel calibration.

**Reference:** Parenty, Moreau, Niel & Campagne 2013, Chem. Rev. ŌĆö macrolactonization survey.

Note: at production the canonical activator is Yamaguchi (TCBC + DMAP +
2 Et3N, ~568 g/mol). Per-substrate alternatives (Shiina/Corey-Nicolaou/
Mukaiyama/EDC/T3P) live in `data/rules/macrolactonization.meta.yaml`.
