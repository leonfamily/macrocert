# lactam_16_from_15_aminopentadecanoic_acid

Surrogate panel case — 16-membered macrolactam from `OC(=O)CCCCCCCCCCCCCCN`.

The structure here is the *target product* (cyclized macrolactam),
not the literature compound; the chemistry is real (industrial
caprolactam-series lactamizations) but the substrate is
simplified for v0 panel calibration.
